Smooth Current Escapes

We are the ultimate destination for adventurers who prefer the softer side of rafting. We don't judge!

Relax with the gentle embrace of nature's waterways while enjoying soothing melodies of smooth jazz. Our purpose is to create unforgettable rafting experiences, where the rhythmic flow of calm rivers harmonize with mellow tunes.

History

Smooth Current Escapes began with just a couple of rafts and a passion for jazz music. As word spread, our company grew, but we retained our mission to provide an escape on the river with the gentle, smooth sounds of jazz. As we continue to grow, our commitment to our original vision remains unchanged. We invite you to join us on the river, where the currents flow smoothly and the music uplifts.
